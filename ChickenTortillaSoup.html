Chicken Tortilla Soup

This easy recipe is courtesy of weightwatchers.com and can make a great meal, side, or snack. Chicken tortilla soup is a great Winter or Summer soup and goes great with some fresh herbs and lime. If you are vegetarian, feel free to cook without the chicken, replace the chicken broth with veggie broth, and just have tortilla soup.

Ingredients

Serves 6 people

  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped onion (sweet or white onion is best)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt (non-kosher salt works too)
  • 4 teaspoons minced garlic (about 3 cloves)
  • 1 jalepeno pepper
  • 6 cups reduced sodium chicken broth
  • 15oz canned diced tomatoes
  • 20oz chicken breast
  • 1/3 cup fresh lime juice (non-fresh works too)
  • 6 small lime slices for garnish
  • 1 cup chopped cilantro + a little for garnish
  • Shredded cheese (chedder of mexican blend)
  • 3 handfulls (about 12) tortilla chips

Preparation

  • Heat olive oil in large pot
  • Add onion and salt until and cook until the onion is transparent (5-10mins)
  • Add garlic, jalepeno, and chili powder. Cook until flavors mingle (1min)
  • Add broth, tomatoes, chicken, and lime juice; stir to combine.
  • Bring to a simmer and cook, covered, until chicken breasts are cooked through (about 20 minutes)
  • Remove chicken from soup and shred with 2 forks; return chicken to pot with cilantro.
  • Serve soup using leftover cilantro and lime to garnish
