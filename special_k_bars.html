Special K Bars

These Special K Bars are a Tufte family tradition. Although I would love to avoid cliche, these are Grandma's recipe. They are gooey, delicous, and melt in your mouth. If you are on a diet, I would avoid these because it is impossible to only eat one. This recipe is courtesy of the late Grandma Doris Tufte, but the image is courtesy of familycookbookproject.com.

Ingredients

Serves 6 people

  • 1 Cup White corn syrup
  • 1 Cup sugar
  • 1 Cup peanut butter
  • 7 Cups slightly crushed cereal K
  • 1 Cup choclate chips
  • 1 Cup butterscotch chips

Preparation

  • Heat syrup and sugar to boiling in a heavy sauce pan
  • Remove from heat and stir in peanut butter
  • Lightly stir in crushed cereal.
  • Spread into a greased 9x13 in. pan
  • In the same heavy sauce pan, melt choclate and butterscotch chips over low heat
  • Stir often, and when completley smooth, spread over top the bars in the 9x13 in. pan
  • Let cool completely then cut into bars and enjoy.
