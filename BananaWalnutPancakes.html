Banana Walnut Pancakes

These Banana Walnut Pancakes are a delicious twist on an easy breakfast. With only four ingredients, these are one of the easiest and most delicous breakfasts you can make. They are perfect for a sleep weekend morning, and they will impress your family and friends without taking hours to make. If you want to make your own pancake batter there is a easy recipe below the pancake recipe.

Ingredients

Serves 3-4 people

  • 1 cup pancake mix of your choosing (Bob's Red Mill is my favorite) or the instructions below
  • 1 cup water or milk (whichever your chosen mix calls for
  • 1 egg (if your mix calls for it)
  • 1 cup Walnut
  • 2 bananas
  • 1 tablespoon Butter + extra for toping
  • Maple Syrup for toping

Preparation

  • First roast the walnuts by placing them in an unoiled pan for 10-15 minutes or until the waxy flavor has left them.
  • Now mix together the pancake mix following the intructions on the pancake mix box or the instructions below
  • Chop a banana horizontally such that you have approximatley 1cm long rounds.
  • Mix walnuts and banana into the pancake mix
  • Cook on each side till golden brown and serve with butter and maple syrup.

Making your own pancake batter

This recipe is courtesy of allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flower
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 1/4 cus milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Preparation

  • Sift together flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar
  • Mix in the milk, egg, and melted butter.
  • ta da your batter is ready. Now follow the recipe above.
