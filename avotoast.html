Smashed Avocado and Egg Toast

No wonder avocado toast is everywhere: It’s a great source of fiber and contains a host of good for-you vitamins, and the crisp/creamy texture satisfies every time.

Ingredients

Serves 1 person

  • ¼ medium, sliced avocado
  • 1 slice(s), or whole grain, toasted whole wheat bread
  • 1 item(s), sliced eggs, hard-boiled/li>
  • 1 pinch table salt
  • 1 pinch black pepper
  • 1 pinch crushed red pepper flakes

Preparation

  • Put avocado slices on toast and gently mash with fork. Arrange sliced egg on top and sprinkle with salt, black pepper, and pepper flakes.
  • Makes 1 serving.
